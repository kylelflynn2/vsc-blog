Analysis 01

$/WAR Surplus Valuation

Each batter's year-to-date WAR is projected to a full season at the team's pace and multiplied by the 2026 market rate ($9.0M / WAR). Surplus value is market value minus estimated salary. Players are then bucketed into Core, Trade-Up, Develop, Hold, or Cut/Replace tiers based on age, projected WAR, and surplus.
